About Better City Resolution
Better City Resolution is a next-generation civic complaint and community engagement platform designed to empower citizens and municipalities alike. It enables transparent issue reporting, grievance tracking, and collaborative problem solving across towns and cities, fostering efficient urban governance.

This is designed to help people resolve their civic issues by connecting them directly with NGOs, fostering effective collaboration for timely solutions and community support.

Benefits to Citizens
A transparent and accountable grievance redressal system for all urban residents.
